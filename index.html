📷 Participatory Photography Study

🧠 Study Title:
Coping and help-seeking among Ukrainian adolescent girls and young women living in the UK since the onset of the Russia-Ukraine war
🎯 Research Purpose:
Join the London School of Hygiene & Tropical Medicine in collaboration with Ukrainian Social Club and the Sanctuary Foundation to help us understand how young Ukrainian women and girls cope and seek support for their mental health.
👥 Eligibility:
Ukrainian girls and women aged 15–24 living in the UK since February 2022
🎁 Compensation:
Receive a £125 voucher after completing all sessions
📍 Location:
In-person workshops (details provided upon sign-up)
🕒 Duration:
Five weekly 1.5-hour workshops over the summer
